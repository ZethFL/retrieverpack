## The Setup

I asked Claude to help me set up a new server. Simple enough, right?

## The Blooper

Claude confidently suggested:

```bash
curl -x chmod 755 myfile.sh
```

When I pointed out that `curl` and `chmod` are... different things, Claude apologized and explained it was "combining the steps for efficiency."

> That's not efficiency, Claude. That's a cry for help.

## The Lesson

AI will confidently combine two completely unrelated commands into one magnificent abomination. It's like asking someone to "drive to the store" and watching them try to steer their refrigerator down the highway.

**Confidence level:** 10/10
**Accuracy level:** Existential crisis/10
